Story

This is my story of CS learning journey. When I was 8 years old, my father bought a 486 computer, it was an amazing machine at that time. Since then, I’ve became a faithful fan of computer and began to learn how to use DOS and Windows for playing games. In high school, I planned to study CS in the university and become a software engineer in the future. Unfortunately, I didn’t get my dream major—CS in the university. I didn’t give up and I learnt CS knowledge every day during undergraduate study, and I got certificates for C Programming Language and Network Engineer. Then, I wanted to learn more knowledge of CS, so I prepared Graduate Entrance Examination and studied very hard everyday after work, finally I was admitted to Software Engineering major and ranked No.1 among all the candidates. During my graduate study, I realized I need go to US and be closer to my dream. After one-year preparation, I achieved that goal, and one-year learning in US proved that my choice is right. Now, I become more knowledgeable and confident.
